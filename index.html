How to Play

Deep Blue Escape — A Magical Ocean Adventure

🐚

Welcome

Race through the magical ocean, overcome underwater challenges, grow stronger, and be the first fish to reach the legendary Golden Pearl.

🏆

Goal

Reach Tile 100 and collect the Golden Pearl before the other player.

😴

Unlocking Your Fish

Your fish begins sleeping. Roll a 3 to wake it up. Only then does your journey begin.

🎲

Rolling the Magical Shell

Click the Magical Shell. The number shown decides how many tiles your fish swims forward.

🌊

Ocean Events

The ocean is alive. During your journey you may encounter:

🦈 Shark 🦀 Crab 🪸 Coral Trap 🌀 Whirlpool 🌋 Volcano ⚡ Jellyfish 🌊 Ocean Current 🪨 Rocks 🐬 Dolphin

Some events help you. Others slow you down. All of them look spectacular.

🐠

Fish Growth

Land on special reward tiles to grow stronger:

🐠 Tiny↓ 🐟 Small↓ 🐡 Large↓ 👑 Giant

Growth is triggered by: 🐬 Dolphin · 🌟 Starfish · 🪨 Pearl · 💎 Treasure Chest

🦀

Shrinking

Two dangers shrink your fish by one level:

🦀 Crab Pinch 🦈 Shark Encounter

Tiny fish cannot shrink further — they just wobble!

🐟

Tail Whack

When two fish land on the same tile, the larger fish performs a playful Tail Whack — pushing the smaller fish backward.

Small → Tiny3 tiles
Large → Tiny5 tiles
Giant → Tiny10 tiles
Large → Small3 tiles
Giant → Small5 tiles
Giant → Large3 tiles

Same size? Funny bump — nobody moves!

🎬

Cinematic Events

Special encounters play as beautiful animated scenes. You can watch or skip after the first moment. First encounters always play in full.

🏆

Winning

The first fish to reach the Golden Pearl wins the adventure! Good luck, little fish! 🐟✨
